Usecases / functionalities of the app.

Delivery app: users can order food and groceries with this app.
restaurants prepare the food and transfer to the delivery partners.
delivery partners deliver the ordered items and get paid.

Customer: 

1.signup / login
2. forgot password
3. select between food or groceries
4. select items
5. checkout
6. place order
7. payment
8. cancel order
9. give rating and feedback for delivery partner and restaurant
10. tip the driver
11. membership

Delivery partner:

1. register / login
2. forgot password
3. accept order
4. skip order
5. pick up details
6. delivery details
7. rewards for completing orders
8. get pay details per delivery
9. get paid by every weekend
10. withdraw amount earned to bankaccount

Restaurant partner:

1. restaurant login
2. change item availability
3. accept order
4. get delivery partner details
5. complete order
